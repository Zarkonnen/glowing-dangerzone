Dark Bibliometrics: Tracking fraud and incompetence in scientific publishing

A proposal for the OKFest 2014 UnFestival.

Big cases of falsified data and crooked peer review have come to light in the last few years. I would like to discuss the idea of a reputation metric for papers, authors, and journals based on closeness to "known bad" science. I would provide a number of concrete examples and a basic proposal for such a metric.
